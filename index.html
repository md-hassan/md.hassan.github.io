Muhammad Yusuf's Page

Hi, I am an incoming MSCS student at UMass Amherst. My research interests include Deep Learning, Image Processing and, Multimodal Learning among others.

Recently, I have been working on 3D vision problems at Dr. Shanmuganathan Raman’s CVIG Lab at IIT Gandhinagar.

I spent last year’s summer hunting for anomalous Astronomical Transients using Machine Learning as a Caltech SURF Fellow, advised by Dr. Asish Mahabal. I have interned before at the University of Calgary as a MITACS GRI scholar with Dr. Roberto Souza. I have also collaborated with Dr. Mayank Singh at IIT Gandhinagar.

Here is my Resume (last updated: July 2024). I am always on the lookout for interesting problems and collaboration opportunities!
